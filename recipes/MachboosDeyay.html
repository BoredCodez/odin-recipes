Machboos Deyay

Delicious Machboos Deyay

A delicious yet simple recipe to make Machboos Deyay for yourself and the family.

Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Hot Sauce
  • Lemon
Steps
  1. Cook the rice and chicken together.
  2. Add hot sauce and lemon to the cooked rice and chicken.
  3. Mix everything up.
  4. Enjoy
